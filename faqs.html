Frequently Asked Questions

1. What types of ice buckets do you offer?

We offer a variety of ice buckets including stainless steel, acrylic, and insulated ice buckets.

2. Can I customize my ice bucket?

Yes, we offer customization options for our ice buckets. Contact us for more information on customization.

3. Do you offer bulk discounts?

Yes, we offer discounts for bulk orders. Please reach out to our sales team for pricing and availability.

4. How do I clean and maintain my ice bucket?

It's recommended to clean your ice bucket with warm, soapy water and a soft cloth. Avoid using abrasive cleaners or scrubbing pads as they may scratch the surface. For stainless steel ice buckets, you can use stainless steel cleaner to maintain shine.
